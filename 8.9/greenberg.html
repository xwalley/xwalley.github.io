Between Love and Obsession:
The Photography of Stanley Greenberg


Greenberg is known for his large scale urban subjects, photographing New York City reservoirs and water systems.
He has been brought on to discuss his works on the B&H Photography Podcast, where he discusses his research and commitment
to explore and photograph the city's sites, including water sources, springs, and wells.
His works are documented and can be viewed on his instagram.
