Light Hunters-Art About the Street:
Clarissa Bonet Street Photography


Clarrisa Bonet is a Chicago based artist whose work explores the physical and psychological issues of urban settings and spaces. She came on the B&H Photography Podcast alongside artist Nina King to discuss her practice in street photography, bringing up the question of if it is possible for artists working in street photography to establish control of the scene. Her work can be viewed on her website.
