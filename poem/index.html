Peace and War In The Illusory Material World

Masnavi:Rumi

Mathnawi I:70-71

70 Within the spirit,1 imagined forms are as nothing --
(yet)
witness an (entire) world
going on
(based) upon something imaginary!

71 (Witness how)
their peace and their war
(is based) upon
something imaginary,
and (how) their pride and their shame
(derives) from something imaginary.
